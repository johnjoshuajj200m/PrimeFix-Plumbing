## Image Quality Guidelines

1. **Resolution:** Use high-quality images (at least 2x the display size for retina screens)
2. **File Size:** Optimize images to keep file sizes reasonable:
   - Hero image: < 300KB
   - Service images: < 150KB each
   - Testimonial images: < 50KB each
3. **Format:** Use JPG for photos (better compression)
4. **Style:** Choose clean, modern, professional images that match the business aesthetic
5. **Color:** Prefer images with good contrast and colors that complement the blue theme
6. **People:** If showing people, ensure they look professional and trustworthy

---

## Recommended Image Optimization Tools

Before uploading, optimize your images using:
- **TinyPNG** (tinypng.com) - Compresses JPG/PNG
- **Squoosh** (squoosh.app) - Google's image optimizer
- **ImageOptim** (imageoptim.com) - Desktop app for Mac
