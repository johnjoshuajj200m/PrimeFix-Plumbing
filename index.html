Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer emergency plumbing services?

Yes! We provide 24/7 emergency plumbing services for urgent issues like burst pipes, major leaks, and sewer backups. Call us anytime at +234-800-PRIMEFIX.

How quickly can you respond to service calls?

We typically respond within 2-4 hours for standard calls and within 60-90 minutes for emergencies. Same-day service is available for most repairs.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we offer free estimates for all plumbing work. We'll assess your situation and provide transparent, upfront pricing before starting any work.

Are your plumbers licensed and insured?

Absolutely. All our plumbers are fully licensed, insured, and background-checked. We maintain proper insurance coverage for your peace of mind.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and major mobile payment platforms. Payment is due upon completion of work unless other arrangements are made.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, all our work comes with a warranty covering both parts and labor. The length and terms depend on the type of service, and we'll explain the warranty details before starting your project.

Project Case Study

This section documents UX and technical decisions for portfolio review.

Goal

Build a high-converting plumbing website that turns visitors into calls and quote requests.

Audience

Homeowners and renters needing fast, trustworthy plumbing service.

Key UX Decisions

  • Emergency strip + mobile call bar to reduce friction
  • Filterable services to help users find the right service fast
  • Trust signals (licensed/insured, warranty, ratings)
  • Simple pricing/estimate messaging to build trust

Technical Decisions

  • Static HTML/CSS/JS for speed and simplicity

Results

  • Responsive across devices
  • SEO-ready structure
  • Clear CTAs and optimized contact flow
